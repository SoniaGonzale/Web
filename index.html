Welcome to our big little family




In this our fault, we offer all those neighbors and non-neighbors of our town to participate in our party. Where you can see that not only the Fallas have to enjoy it and you can see how it is not only the Fallas week, but there is much more underneath all that..


Every year during the Fallas week from March 15 to 19 we celebrate the special Fallas week, a tradition that has been around since the 18th century 1740.


They originate from an ancient custom of carpenters who, celebrating the arrival of the first, on the night of March 19, burned in front of their workshops the pieces of wood (parots) that they used to raise the lamps that illuminated them during the winter..


Little by little, they added old junk and rags to this purifying fire, which humanized the parots until they became ninots. The smoke of the Valencians soon gave those ninots the critical and ironic sense that they maintain today..


We must mention that today, fallaes have evolved to become those ephemeral works of art that, in some cases, have million-dollar budgets.


We also remember that our failures in general, our festivities in 2016, were inscribed on the Representative List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity of UNESCO.


For all this, we invite you to (gaudisque) our traditions and enjoy them, until the end.
